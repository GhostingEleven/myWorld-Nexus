Privacy Policy — myWorld Nexus

Effective date: 2025

myWorld Nexus does not collect, store, share, or process personal data. The app provides an offline-friendly reading experience without accounts, tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. No names, emails, device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage analytics are collected by the app.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app does not integrate ad networks, analytics services, or social SDKs.

Payments

Payments (e.g., content unlocks, tips/donations) are processed by third-party providers such as Stripe or Google Play Billing. Payment details are handled by those providers and are not stored by myWorld Nexus.

Device Permissions

The app does not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, photos, location, or files.

Children

This app is intended for a mature audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

As no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk within the app. Payment flows rely on the security practices of their respective providers.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s functionality changes to involve data collection, this policy will be updated before such changes take effect.
